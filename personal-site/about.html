My work at I-CCEW

I work at the Ronnie K. Irani Center for the Creation of Economic Wealth (I-CCEW), where I'm the Director of our Oklahoma City offices. I-CCEW works to develop the entrepreneurial ecosystem of Oklahoma through programming related to innovation, technology, social entrepreneurship, and more.

My work with OK Coders

OK Coders is Oklahoma's first developer bootcamp. We teach 4- to 8-week introductory bootcamps to adults in OKC and Tulsa who are interested in getting their feet wet with web development. OK Coders bootcamps teach primarily the MEAN stack, but we've also taught React and are experimenting with teaching data analytics using R and SQL.
